I P P

The wheels we sell are hand selected by our team of professionals and are made from the finest materials. We source all our alloy wheels from Europe's first-class manufacturers and we guarantee our wheels for the 10 years. We have a variety of styles and sizes that will suit every car. Our wheels are light weight and help brake-cooling by providing exceptional breathing spaces. Our wheels make your car look good and safer to drive!

Excalibur

Excalibur is suitable for road and track. Lightweight magnesium-aluminium alloy allows supreme brake cooling.


Flamer

Flamer is appropriate for both on and off-road driving. Medium-weight nickel-aluminium alloy provides a tough exterior.

Kraken

Kraken is an upmarket road-worthy wheel. Medium-weight nickel-carbon alloy suited for high-end vehicles.


Mogul

Mogul is light-weight road and track wheel. The carbon-magnesium alloy permits superior cooling.


Piercer

Piercer is a high-end race-track wheel. With a lightweight carbon-fibre alloy giving an extra edge on the track.


Scotia

Scotia is the most popular of the on-road designs. Manufactured with magnesium alloy giving excellent cooling power.

Senator

Senator is suitable for all driving types. Lightweight nickel-aluminium alloy provides a scratch-resistant exterior.

Tyro

Tyro was built for track and road. A light-weight carbon-fibre alloy will help give you the leading edge on the track.
